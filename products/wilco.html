WILCO

WILCO is a tool suite without any on-board installation that makes it possible to monitor in proactive and curative ways all the systems of your airplanes to optimize its performances and availability.

Request a demo Ask us for a live demo! we can set up a demo private account for you to try for free!

WILCO provides a complete collection of tools to help airlines avoiding dispatch delays and improving operational efficiency. Flight data, queried remotely to the in-flight aircraft using ACARS communication stream, is analysed to immediately generate readable reports and discover opportunities for improved efficiency.

WILCO offers a web-based in-flight Data Analysis Service which brings together a comprehensive suite of tools and expertise to unlock the value from airlinesâ€™flight data several hours before the coming dispatch. This saves airlines from dealing with rush hours during the dispatch, providing direct access to the very best analysts and methodologies available today while the aircraft is flying. The value of the tool suite will be further enhanced by Flightwatching's unique position to customize smart rules and computation models (called IFTs in the product).

  • Focus on your core business
  • Lower training, maintenance and operational costs
  • Access to group of experts with operational experience
  • Airline maintains full ownership and access to its flight data
  • Benefit from WILCO economies of scale, lower set-up and on-going costs
  • Rely upon a neutral and highly trusted organization
  • Be part of a network of industry leading flight data analysts from aircraft manufacturers, suppliers and airlines
Special features:
  • A secured Web-based allows airline team to access results anytime, anywhere in the world where there is internet access
  • Complex report support including full flight monitoring
  • High end reports to be easily downloaded for airline review
  • Subscription to on-event data requests with e-mail/SMS notifications (ex:ECAM warning, OOOI events...)
  • Access to statistics, graphs and trend monitoring
