Quick prototyping

Just draw your schematic with standard tools (like Illustrator), reuse PDF files, upload it to WILCO and it gets animated with your data!

Proprietary data connectivity

WILCO layout helper allows you to describe your data and map it to parameters. For complex data, you can create your own adapter using the WILCO API. WILCO comes with some plugins to read CSVs, ACARS data and more...

on the fly analysis and alerts

We call them IFTs (If This Then That). Create simple rules that runs each time a data is received and trig an action: send an e-mail, a SMS, command your system, make a request and more... The IFTs can be activated or desactivated in one click!

Latest WEB technologies

WILCO uses cutting-edge technology to bring live system visualization to the technicians or engineers in your browser, your tablet or your phone without any client-side installation

Stunning dashboards

The dashboards are interactive. Click on a gauge, you have access to all the relevant graphs. Click on a dot, WILCO brings you to the context of the dot. Get access to the infinite timeline of events and filter them.

Secured access

WILCO is based on secured HTTPS access. You can manage your users and their the access rights using the User admin page

Easy integration

Wilco is open. all the data is accessible using a restful JSON API. you can integrate it into your information system to push or pull information.

Experts on-board

We have experts in IT to help you decoding your data and use our tool. We have experts in aeronautics to decode or program ACMS reports, to handle QARs and to use 100% of ARINC-620 stardards
