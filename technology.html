Fetch your aircraft systems data in-flight

FlightWatching is an innovative company, awarded by the French Minister of Research in 2013 creating new software standards for the Aerospace market.

Newbies Aircraft can send data to the ground thru satellite or VHF radio communication. The messages can be trigged either automatically or according to ground/crew request. Flightwatching uses this ability to ease dispatch, optimize maintenance activities and track actual aircraft routes.

Engineering

FlightWatching provides instant performance analysis and trend monitoring on all aircraft systems.

Control centers

FlightWatching provides preventive maintenance solutions. Automatic requests monitor systems to detect risks of delays in the future.

Line maintenance

Flightwatching remotely digs into the aircraft sytem to find the root cause of a fault while the aircraft is flying. No fault found situations are significantly reduced.
