Query your aircraft systems in-flight

Aircraft can send data to the ground thru satellite or VHF radio communication. The messages can be trigged either automatically either according to ground or crew request. Flightwatching uses this ability to easy dispatch, optimize maintenance activities and track actual aircraft route.

Maintenance usecases

FlightWatching ground analysis technology automatically watches and queries Boeing and Airbus fleet. The collected data helps the airline to prepare the tools/spares and shedule the right team at the gate while the aircraft is in-flight.

Engineering usecases

Flightwatching collects all the data into a big-data database where predefined or customized queries enables visualizing instant analysis.

Control centers

FlightWatching provides stunning live dashboard for every aircraft. Regular queries tracks the real position of the aircraft and warns the officers in case of drift from the flight plan.
