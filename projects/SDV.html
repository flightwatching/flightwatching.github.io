About the project

Lean methods have been applied to iteratively integrate and deploy WILCO within a local network a WEB application. WILCO is fully integrated in a wider application: The e-Squadron Maintenance (ESM) developped by Dassault Aviation has been introduced at ADS SHOW 2014.

Rafale data management

WILCO provides interactive dashboards based on Rafale military data. The application processes the maintenance data of the Rafale and renders it as graphics. The data becomes easy to read for the maintenance teams and the headquarter.
