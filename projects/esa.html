Where is Rosetta
play around with the probe. watch it full-screen

Here is an example of a realization with WILCO. The initial computation of interactions may take a few seconds to run. When it is done, you can do several things with the demonstrator and understand the followed path of Rosetta to land on 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko comet

Travel in time Click and drag on the timeline at the top of the solar system and display the exact situation at the selected date

Travel in space Click and drag on the solar system to pan it, scroll up or down to zoom

Click on a planet display the graphs of the position over time. When on a graph, click on a parameter name to remove it from the graph, click and drag to zoom, double-click to auto-scale and shift-click-drag to pan the curves. For example, click on the earth (the blue planet), remove earth_x and earth_y to see the influence of the moon on the vertical earth axis

Click on Rosetta display the evolution of the distance to earth among time

Click on the comet display the evolution of the speed of the comet
